Grilled Cheese Recipe

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Bread
  • Tender, Love and Care
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Butter Bread
  2. Slice cheese
  3. Face buttered side out and put in sliced cheese
  4. Grilled both slides on a griddle or in a frying pan till golden brown
